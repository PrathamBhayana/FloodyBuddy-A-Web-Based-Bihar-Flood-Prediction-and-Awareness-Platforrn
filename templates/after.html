SAFETY MEASURES

    BEFORE FLOOD
  • Prepare an emergency kit: Include essentials like water, food, flashlight, batteries, first aid, medicines, and important documents.
  • Know your area: Be aware if you live in a flood-prone zone and identify higher ground or safe shelters nearby.
  • Secure your home: Elevate electrical appliances, seal basements, and unplug devices to prevent electrical accidents.
    DURING FLOOD
  • Evacuate if advised: Do not delay evacuation if authorities recommend it.
  • Stay on higher ground: Move to the roof or upper floors if trapped.
  • Turn off electricity: If water starts entering your home, switch off the main power.
  • Stay connected: Use a battery-powered radio or phone to receive alerts and updates
    AFTER FLOOD
  • Do not return immediately: Wait for official clearance before going back to your home.
  • Beware of contamination: Avoid using floodwater for drinking or cleaning. Boil water before use.
  • Watch for hazards: Look out for snakes, exposed wires, or unstable structures.
  • Seek medical help: If you or anyone is injured or exposed to contaminated water.
